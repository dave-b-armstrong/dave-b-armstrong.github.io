Sabbatical: Week Four

December 27, 2024 by Dave Armstrong

This was a low-key week. We did the annual Christmas-adjacent birthday, we exchanged some presents, and we also had all the kids home all week, sometimes with extras. And it was snowy and icy and we spent a lot of time just holed up relaxing.

So there is not a ton to report this week. My exercise was paused, my digital work was paused. My printing work did progress - I found a bunch of ways to fail at transferring images, and one that worked, so that is progress. I did a pile of research and thinking about art in general and where I want my creative practice to fall. 

Next week is still vacation-like, with kids still home from school, but probably more productivity. Hopefully.
