Spouse-a-rific and I are Joining Stores

February 16, 2024 by Dave Armstrong

Loretta, queen of the universe, crafter extraordinaire, primary creator of all things crafty... is going to join me in my quest to sell share our creations. Because that is how we buy more supplies for more things - share them with the world.

To that end, the store is going to be sketchy for the weekend. Changing designs, domains, info architecture (menus), and all kinds of things.

I say this less because anyone will see it while it is broken and more because I have been doing software too long to comfortably go to bed knowing that a site I own is wonky. 

Well, it is. The 0-1 of you who notice should check back next week!
