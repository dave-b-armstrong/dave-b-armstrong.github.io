Starting VR Dev

November 11, 2024 by Dave Armstrong

I am starting to learn VR dev in order to create my own workout games for the long cold Wisconsin winters. And I'm having a blast doing it. Bringing 30 years of software experience onto a new platform, learning, creating, without any pressure to do anything other than what I want... it brings back the joy I had decades ago when I first started doing software.

I fully recognize my complete noob status at VR, and at gaming, so I doubt anything I post on this topic is going to be useful to people who have a clue. But I'm going to be doing it anyway because it will help me to remember things. And who knows... maybe I can actually build a few tools or games that are worth sharing.
